Welcome!

We see ourselves capable of accomplishing great things and becoming better people than we ever thought we could on our own through the teachings of Lambda Chi Alpha and the friendships that we build with each other. We strive to develop ourselves, improve our communities, and to become more of a brother towards one another.

We see each other as an extension of our family that we can count on for anything. Our bond extends beyond our local chapter and time in college to the world where we can have a conversation with a brother that attended a university on the other side of the country. We believe in the same core values, have taken the same oaths, and share a mutual connection as brothers.
